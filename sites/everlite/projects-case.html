While working with Bangarra dance theatre in Barangaroo, we provided a combination of DALI controllable Wall lights, Linear luminaires and Panels.
With the combination of these products a clever, thoughtful and highly versatile lighting concept was created to cater to the requirements of each of the different areas throughout the project.
